Okay. My first (serious) talk idea. Would you attend?

The Hitchhikers Guide to Strategic vs. Technical: A Babel Fish for translating between cyber management and operators

Basically, we all know there is a cultural divide between management (i.e. CEO/CISO, that sort of level) and those doing the actual ops (and by that I include things like GRC, DevSecOps, etc..). And we perpetuate it by having ops talk to ops and management to management at cons.

So, how about a talk with two versions. One to be delivered at a management level con, one at a ops con. Both structured to highlight to the attendees how to work with the attendees of the other.

The key point being good tips and lessons, but also ensuring it's a two way street. For example, cyber ops should talk in risk, but should management learn how to talk cyber too?

Thoughts?
