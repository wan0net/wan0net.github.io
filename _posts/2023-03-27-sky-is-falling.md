The problem with advising people to constantly be "ON ALERT" is it's like the DFS sofa that's always on sale. Or the IT incident tickets that are always set to P1. You take away the ability for people to prioritise, to effectively manage peaks and troughs, by saying there are no troughs, and to just calm down for a second. No wonder cyber professionals always feel like it's crunch time and they get burnt out so hard.

Stop using international politics to sell your services because it is going to cause an effect on everyone else delivering those services having to work 150% more wasting their time.
