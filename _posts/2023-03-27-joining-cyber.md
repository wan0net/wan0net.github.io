Stop setting up panels or discussions where we say that we need education to bring people into Cyber. I think that's been pretty bleeding obvious since I got into Cyber... and that was 10 years ago.

Be the person who starts doing. Start mentoring people. Teaching at universities or TAFE's on a part time basis. Supporting communities of people learning or working together. Building job roles that can support graduates who leave the education and then are not expected to be experts.

There's a lot of doing we can all be achieving, yet I feel like a lot of us are still just talking about the problem.
