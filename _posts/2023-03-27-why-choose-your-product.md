I get a lot of vendors asking me to look at their products, and ultimately I'm going to respond with three specific questions to determine if I'm going to investigate your product further:

1. What is your value proposition or differentiation against your competitors? (If you tell me there is no competition then I'm going to be highly sceptical)
2. How well do you integrate with other vendors technology?
3. Are you on Prem, or Cloud. If Cloud, are you IRAP assessed (all my clients are Fed Gov, so if I moved to commercial this question might change)

With cyber products being a commodity now it's important to be able to distinguish yourself in a true sense rather than just marketing, and to ensure you are fit for purpose. I don't want to waste your time if there are incompatibilities in these areas.
